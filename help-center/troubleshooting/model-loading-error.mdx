---
title: AI Model Not Loading
description: Quick fix for Sovadina AI model loading issues.
---

## Symptoms

- App displays "Model failed to load"
- No AI results returned

## Causes

- Outdated dependencies
- Network issues

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Restart the Sovadina server">
    ````bash
    systemctl restart sovadina
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update AI dependencies">
    ````bash
    pip install --upgrade sovadina-ai
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
