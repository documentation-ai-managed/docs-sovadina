## Overview

Generate structured notes from raw medical transcripts.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload transcript">
    In Sovadina dashboard, click "New Note" and upload your audio or text file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run AI extraction">
    Select "Auto Extract" to generate structured summary via AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & export">
    Edit auto-generated note as needed. Export in FHIR or PDF format.
  </Step>
</Steps>
